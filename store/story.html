Woven with
Purpose

Verse & Thread started with a simple idea: that the clothes we wear should reflect the truths we carry.

Each hoodie in our collection is more than just fabric; it's a prompt to pause, reflect, and remember the promises of Scripture. We believe that what you wear can be a silent testimony and a personal reminder of grace throughout your day.

Our embroidery is done with precision and care, ensuring that your favorite verse lasts as long as the garment itself. By choosing heavyweight 100% cotton and high-density stitching, we prioritize quality over quantity and purpose over fast fashion.

This isn't just apparel. It's Verse & Thread.
